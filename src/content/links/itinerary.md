**Attention!** This is not a perfect itinerary. There are some parts that can be changed/should be changed. The following table is simply the actual itinerary of my trip. Do read my accompanying blogs for the reviews of the places I went to, or reach out to me directly if you have any question.

Also, this itinerary may omit some food places, because I cannot remember all the restaurants I went to :)

You may notice that this itinerary is somewhat packed. Yes, it is more suitable for solo travellers, or if your group travels very fast.
